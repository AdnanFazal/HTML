The structure or body of any document consist of heading, sub heading and paragraphs.
To describe the structure of a web page, we add codes to the words we want to appear on the page.
Following is the general structure of a web page

      <!DOCTYPE html>
      <html lang="en">
      <head>
        <meta charset="UTF-8">
        <title>Document</title>
      </head>
      <body>

      </body>
      </html>

  • HTML codes are made up of characters that live inside angled brackets e.g. <p></p>
